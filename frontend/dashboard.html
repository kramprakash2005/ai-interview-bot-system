Autonomous Interview Platform

Workflow Intelligence

Mirai is an end-to-end recruitment engine designed to eliminate manual technical screening through high-fidelity AI automation.

Automated Initialization

Interviews are created by feeding job descriptions directly into the engine. Mirai parses core competencies to generate a custom evaluation logic tailored to that specific role.

Dynamic Execution

Once deployed, your virtual interviewer, Tachibana Hinata, engages candidates in a conversational dialogue that adapts in real-time based on the depth of their technical responses.

Create Session

Deploy a new interview session. Mirai will generate evaluation logic automatically.

View Sessions

Manage existing interview sessions, review participants, and access AI reports.
